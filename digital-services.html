Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our digital services

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. For educational payments, we also accept payment through our secure online portal.

How long does printing take?

Most printing jobs are completed within 30 minutes. Large projects or binding services may take 1-2 hours depending on complexity.

Do you offer online training?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online training sessions. Online classes are conducted via Zoom with recorded sessions available for review.

Can I get same-day passport photos?

Absolutely! Passport photos are typically ready within 15-30 minutes. We ensure they meet all official requirements.

Do you provide warranty on devices?

Yes, all brand new devices come with manufacturer warranty. UK used laptops come with our 3-month warranty.

Can you help with JAMB registration?

Yes, we handle complete JAMB registration including form purchase, profile creation, and center selection. Bring your required documents and we'll take care of the rest.
