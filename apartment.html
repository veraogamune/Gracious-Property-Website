Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our apartments

How do I book an apartment?

Complete the booking form above, upload required documents, and submit for approval. Once approved, you'll receive payment instructions.

What is the payment structure?

Initial payment covers 2 months rent plus registration fee (₦7,000), caution fee (₦5,000) and gas fee - 2 months (₦6,000). Subsequent payments are monthly.

What documents do I need?

You need a valid ID card (National ID, Driver's License, or Passport) and a recent passport photograph.

Are utilities included in the rent?

Yes! Electricity, water, bedspreads, kitchen utensils, gas cylinders and 24/7 security with many more are all included in your monthly rent.

Can I visit before booking?

Absolutely! Contact us to schedule a viewing at any of our locations.

What happens after I submit my booking?

Your request goes to our team for review. We'll contact you within 24-48 hours with approval status and payment details.
